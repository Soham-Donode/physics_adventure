🎯 Cannon Control

Calculate the right angle and speed to eliminate the enemies on the right side!

Projectile equations:
x(t) = v·cos(θ)·t
y(t) = v·sin(θ)·t − ½g·t²
Range = v²·sin(2θ)/g  (g = 9.8 m/s²)
